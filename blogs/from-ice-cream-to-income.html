From Ice Cream to Income: What Selling on Amazon Taught Me

October 15, 2025

Two years scooping ice cream and a year flipping products on Amazon gave me the same lesson: consistency wins. Whether it's managing ROI spreadsheets or debugging TypeScript, success comes from iteration, not perfection.

At the ice cream shop, I got incrementally better at small things every shift. With Amazon FBA, I applied the same approach—start small, test different products, track what works, and iterate. Most listings failed, but each one taught me something valuable.

Now I apply this to coding. I don't aim for perfect code on the first try. I write something that works, test it, refactor it, and repeat. Ship the MVP, get feedback, iterate, and compound those small wins.
